Masters Thesis

  • Title : Research on Chinese Medical Question Answering Based on Deep Neural Networks
  • Supervisor: Professor Hui Wang
  • Description: This thesis aims to study Chinese community question-answering techniques for the medical field. This paper uses the representation of character embeddings to relieve the problem of a great deal of medical terminology in Chinese medical texts. Also, two different semantic matching models are proposed in the paper to tackle the issue of “semantic gap”. Experimental results illustrate that methods presented in the paper show a sharp increase in top-1 accuracy when compared to previous methods.
